Company Intro

Symposius aims to incorporate AI-powered algorithms into personalized dining recommendations, reducing misleading impressions from restaurant marketing. The platform also aims to foster a dynamic community where users can share their discoveries, creating a trusted network of authentic dining experiences.

Mission

  • Accurate and continuously improving AI tools for bespoke and comprehensive restaurant recommendations
  • Authentic, real-time user ratings that reflect a dynamic score for each restaurant
  • A social platform among acquaintances, instead of KOLs, where users engage with their friend groups, sharing dining experiences and personalized preferences
